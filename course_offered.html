Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

3 Years

B.Com is an undergraduate program that provides foundational knowledge in accounting,finance,business and economics.

Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS)

3 Years

B.Com in (Management Studies) is an undergraduate program focusing on management,leadership and business strategies.

B.Sc (Information Technology)

3 Years

B.Sc in (Information Technology) is an undergraduate program that focuses on programming,networking and databases.

B.Com (Accounting & Finance)

3 Years

B.Com in (Accounting and Finance) is an undergraduate program that combines accounting,finance,taxation and auditing.

B.Com (Banking & Insurance)

3 Years

B.Com in (Banking and Insurance) is an undergraduate program focusing on banking,finance,insurance and risk management.

B.Com (Financial Markets)

3 Years

B.Com in (Financial Markets) is a 3-year undergraduate program that focuses on financial theory, investment strategies, risk management, and market operations.

B.Com Hons. (International Accounting)

3 Years

B.Com(Hons) in (International Accounting) is a 3-year undergraduate degree that offers in-depth knowledge of commerce, accounting, economics, finance, and business law.

B.Sc Artificial Intelligence

3 Years

B.Sc in (Artificial Intelligence) is a three-year undergraduate program that blends computer science, machine learning, and data science to build intelligent systems.

Postgraduate Programs

M.Com (Accountancy)

2 Years

M.Com in (Accountancy) is a 2-year postgraduate degree focusing on advanced studies in commerce, accounting, finance, economics, and business management.

M.Com (Banking And Finance)

2 Years

M.Com in (Banking and Finance) is a 2-year postgraduate program that provides specialized knowledge in banking systems, financial markets, risk management, investment analysis, and monetary policies.

M.Sc (Information Technology)

2 Years

M.Sc in (Information Technology) is a 2-year postgraduate program focused on advanced concepts in software development, data management, networking, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

M.Sc (Data Science)

2 Years

M.Sc in (Data Science) is a 2-year postgraduate program that focuses on data analysis, machine learning, statistics, programming, and big data technologies.

M.Com (Global Wealth Management)

2 Years

M.Com in (Global Wealth Management) is a 2-year postgraduate program that focuses on managing high-net-worth individual (HNWI) portfolios, international finance, investment strategies, estate planning, and financial advisory.

M.Com (Financial Research And Technical Analysis)

2 Years

M.Com in (Financial Research and Technical Analysis) is a 2-year postgraduate program that combines in-depth financial research methods with practical technical analysis tools.

M.Com (Business Management)

2 Years

M.Com in (Business Management) is a 2-year postgraduate program that focuses on advanced concepts in organizational management, business strategy, leadership, marketing, and finance.

Research Programs

Ph.D. (Commerce)

3-5 Years

Ph.D. in (Commerce) is a doctoral research program that involves original, in-depth study in areas like finance, accounting, marketing, business management, or economics.
